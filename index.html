What is the Solar System?

The Solar System is made up of the Sun, eight planets, moons, asteroids, comets, and other space objects. The Sun is the center of the Solar System and provides light and heat to all the planets.

The Eight Planets

The planets are Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, and Neptune. Earth is the only known planet that supports life. Pluto was once considered a planet but is now a planet dwarf planet.
